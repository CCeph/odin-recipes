Mediterranean Chicken and Vegetables

Description

This delicious and healthy recipe is cooked in a slow cooker, so it is very easy and convenient to prepare. Despite the dish taking about five and a half hours to cook, you will only really spend about an hour in the kitchen. Note that if you prefer, you can replace the rice in this recipe with couscous or pita bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 8 bone-in chicken thighs, skin removed
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 12 marinated artichoke hearts, drained
  • 4 large carrots, chopped
  • 4 large garlic cloves, halved
  • 1 (3 inch) piece cinnamon stick
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, or more as needed
  • 1 large sweet onion, halved and thinly sliced
  • ½ pound green beans, trimmed and halved
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • ¼ cup coarsely chopped cilantro
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 cups rice

Steps

  1. Mix 1 tsp of tumeric, ginger, coriander, salt, and ¼ tsp of cumin and cayenne pepper in a small cup.
  2. Rub the mixture over the chicken and let it sit for at least 30 mins
