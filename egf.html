Egg Fried rice

Description

Arguably the easiest main dish to ever exist, egg fried rice. Depending on how good you are, you can be in and out of the kitchen with this delicious dish in as little as 15 minutes. If you are one with the noodles, I strongly suggest giving this a try as you're getting a much more balanced meal for little extra effort. Note that this recipe assumes (and recommends) that you already have some cooked rice from before. However, if you do not have leftover rice, just steam some rice prior to following the steps on this recipe. There is no excuse not to cook when this dish exists!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 cup of frozen mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Steam some rice if there is no leftover rice from before.
  2. Preheat a medium-sized pan at medium heat.
  3. While the pan is preheating, whisk 3 eggs.
  4. Add 1 tsp of olive oil when the pan is preheated. Let the oil heat up on the pan for about 15 seconds.
  5. Add 3 whisked eggs to the pan and scramble them.
  6. Add 1 cup of rice and mix together.
  7. Then, add the cup of frozen mixed vegetables and a touch of soy sauce.
  8. Mix again and let it cook for 5-10 minutes.
  9. Serve.
