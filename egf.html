Egg Fried rice

Description

Arguably the easiest main dish to ever exist, egg fried rice. Depending on how good you are, you can be in and out of the kitchen with this delicious dish in as little as 15 minutes. If you are one with the noodles, I strongly suggest giving this a try as you're getting a much more balanced meal for little extra effort. There is no excuse not to cook when this dish exists!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce
